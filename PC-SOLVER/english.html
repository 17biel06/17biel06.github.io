PC Diagnostic Tool

1. Does the PC boot?

2. Check the power supply. Is the power supply okay?

3. Check if the hard drive light is working. Is the light on or works properly?

4. Press the Num Lock key to see if the indicator light turns on and off.

5. Are there signs of functionality?

6. Check the power cord. Is the cable okay?

7. Check the power switch on the back (if there is one). Is the switch on?

8. Check the internal wiring. Is the wiring okay?

9. Check the motherboard and CPU. Are the motherboard and CPU okay?

10. PC boots but no image?

11. Check the monitor connection. Is the connection okay?

12. Check the monitor's power supply. Is the power supply okay?

13. Check the graphics card. Is the graphics card okay?

14. Does the PC boot up but shut down immediately?

15. Check the ventilation and CPU temperature. Are the ventilation and temperature okay?

16. Check the power supply. Is the power supply okay?

17. Does the PC boot up but the operating system does not boot?

18. Check the BIOS settings. Is the BIOS setup okay?

19. Check the hard drive. Is the hard drive okay?

20. Check the operating system installation. Is the operating system installation okay?
