PC Diagnostic Tool

1. Does the PC start?

2. Check the power supply. Is the power supply okay?

3. Check if the hard drive light is on. Is the light on or functional?

4. Press the Num Lock key to see if the light indicator turns on and off.

5. Are there signs of functionality?

6. Check the power cord. Is the cord okay?

7. Check the power switch at the back (if any). Is the switch on?

8. Check the internal wiring. Is the wiring okay?

9. Check the motherboard and CPU. Are the motherboard and CPU okay?

10. Does the PC start but there is no image?

11. Check the monitor connection. Is the connection okay?

12. Check the monitor's power supply. Is the power supply okay?

13. Check the graphic card. Is the graphic card okay?

14. Does the PC start but shut down immediately?

15. Check the cooling system and CPU temperature. Are they okay?

16. Check the power supply. Is it correct?

17. ¿The PC turns on but the operating system does not load?

18. Check the BIOS settings. Are they correct?

19. Check the hard drive. Is it correct?

20. Check the operating system installation. Is the operating system installation correct?
